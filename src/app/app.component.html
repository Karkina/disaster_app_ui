Why Study Natural Disasters?

Natural disasters, such as earthquakes, hurricanes, and wildfires, have a profound impact on our planet and society. By understanding these events, we can:

  • Protect lives and property through early warning systems.
  • Prepare communities for disaster response and recovery.
  • Advance scientific research to mitigate future disasters.

Join us in exploring the forces of nature and their significance for our world.
